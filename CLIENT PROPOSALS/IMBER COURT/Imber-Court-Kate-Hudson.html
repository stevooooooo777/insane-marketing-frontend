The Challenge Every Large Venue Faces

Managing multiple spaces with limited visibility

❌ No Real-Time Visibility

Staff can't see which tables need attention across different areas. Guests wait unnecessarily while staff are elsewhere.

❌ Inefficient Communication

Guests trying to get attention by waving, walking to the bar, or waiting frustrated. Staff running between areas.

❌ Lost Revenue Opportunities

Tables ready to order desserts or drinks are missed. Peak times mean slower service and fewer turns.

❌ Operational Blind Spots

No data on table performance, service timing, or staff efficiency. Decisions made on gut feel, not facts.

❌ Event Coordination Issues

Private events require dedicated staff attention. Hard to balance event service with regular operations.

❌ High Staff Training Costs

New staff need constant supervision. No system to help them prioritize and manage their sections efficiently.

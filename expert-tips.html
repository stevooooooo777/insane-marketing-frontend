QR Code Placement & Conversion
Maximize scan rates and engagement
  • Eye-Level Positioning
    Place QR codes at eye level when customers are seated. Avoid flat placement on tables where they're hard to notice.
    +35% scan rate
  • Clear Call-to-Action
    Use specific text like "Scan for Menu" or "Tap for WiFi" instead of bare QR codes. Context increases engagement.
    +50% completion rate
  • WiFi at Entry
    Position WiFi QR codes near entrance/host stand so customers connect immediately upon arrival.
    Longer stays
  • Review Timing
    Present review QR codes at payment point when customers are satisfied, not during the meal.
    +60% review rate
Optimal Service Timing
When to approach and interact
  • Menu Alert Response
    Approach within 2 minutes when system alerts you to multiple menu scans - customers are ready to order.
    Critical timing
  • Post-Delivery Check
    Wait 8-12 minutes after food delivery before checking back. Customers need time to taste and settle.
    Proper pacing
  • Bill Presentation
    Present the check within 5 minutes of customers finishing their last course, not before.
    +25% tip average
  • Dessert Window
    Offer dessert before clearing main course plates. Visual cues influence purchase decisions.
    +30% dessert sales
Customer Conversation
What to say and when to say it
  • Assumptive Opening
    Ask "What can I start you with to drink?" instead of "Are you ready to order?" - assumes the sale.
    Faster ordering
  • Specific Suggestions
    Use "Would you like to try our [specific item]?" rather than "Any appetizers?" Specific converts better.
    +40% upsell rate
  • "What's Good" Response
    When asked "What's good?", enthusiastically recommend 2-3 highest margin items with personal touch.
    Higher profitability
  • Order Confirmation
    Always repeat orders back to avoid kitchen mistakes and show attentiveness to detail.
    Fewer errors
System Intelligence Use
Leveraging data for better service
  • Regular Dashboard Checks
    Check mobile dashboard every 10-15 minutes during service. Don't wait for urgent alerts.
    Proactive service
  • Alert Prioritization
    Customer service button alerts always take priority over behavioral predictions.
    Customer satisfaction
  • Session Duration Intelligence
    Use table session data to gauge when customers might want dessert or the bill.
    Perfect timing
  • WiFi Connection Patterns
    Customers who connect immediately tend to stay longer - great upsell opportunities.
    Longer visits
Revenue Maximization
Increase average ticket size
  • Wine Pairing Suggestions
    Suggest wine pairings for entrees. Even 20% uptake significantly boosts average ticket.
    +15% average ticket
  • Strategic Bundling
    "Would you like to add a side salad to make that a complete meal?" Bundle items naturally.
    Higher margins
  • Premium Positioning
    Present premium options first, then standard. Customers often choose the first acceptable option.
    Premium sales lift
  • Data-Driven Attention
    Use system data to identify best customers and provide extra attention to build loyalty.
    Customer retention
Peak Time Management
Handling rush periods effectively
  • Pre-Rush Preparation
    Use QR scan patterns to predict busy periods. Position staff 15 minutes before expected rushes.
    Smooth service
  • Table Intelligence Seating
    Use table turnover data to optimize seating. Balance server sections based on real activity.
    Even workload
  • Streamlined Communication
    During rushes, use system alerts instead of verbal updates. Keep kitchen communication efficient.
    Reduced errors
  • Priority Queue Management
    Process service requests in order of urgency shown in system, not arrival order.
    Customer satisfaction

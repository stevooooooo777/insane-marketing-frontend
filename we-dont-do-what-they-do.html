The Full Picture: What Their Privacy Policies Actually Say

Salesforce: Extensive Data Sharing Ecosystem

From Salesforce's Privacy Policy:
"We may share Personal Data (in the form of identifiers and internet activity information) with third party advertisers for purposes of targeting advertisements on non-Salesforce websites, applications and services."

Marketing & Advertising Partners

  • Third-party social media networks (Facebook, LinkedIn, etc.)
  • Advertising networks for targeted ads
  • Websites for marketing campaigns
  • Data shared to "market and advertise on third party platforms"

Business Partners & Affiliates

  • AppExchange partners who may contact you
  • Contest and promotion sponsors
  • Event and webinar sponsors (badge scanning)
  • Partners offering "supplementary services"
  • Global corporate affiliates

Service Providers

  • IT and system administration
  • Credit card processing
  • Research and analytics companies
  • Marketing service providers
  • Customer support vendors
  • Data enrichment services

What They Collect & Share

  • Identifiers (names, emails, contact info)
  • Internet activity information
  • Professional/employment data
  • Financial account information
  • Commercial information
  • Visual information

Oracle: Global Data Processing Network

From Oracle's Privacy Policy:
"Oracle is a global corporation with operations in over 80 countries and personal information is processed globally as necessary in accordance with this policy."

Third-Party Partners

  • Oracle distributors and resellers
  • Specific partners offering "complementary products"
  • Third parties "to facilitate interest-based advertising"
  • Event partners and conference sponsors
  • Data aggregators (who may have no direct relationship with you)

Service Providers

  • IT service providers
  • Credit card and payment processors
  • Order fulfillment companies
  • Analytics providers
  • Event/campaign management
  • Website management
  • E-mail delivery services
  • Auditing firms

Global Data Transfers

  • Shared throughout Oracle's worldwide organization (80+ countries)
  • Processed in countries "that may not provide the same level of protection"
  • Transferred to Oracle affiliates globally
  • Third-party subprocessors in multiple jurisdictions

What They Collect From Third Parties

  • Information from "selected third party sources"
  • Data aggregators who have no relationship with you
  • Third parties who collect on Oracle's behalf
  • Publicly available information about you

Understanding Service Alerts

Types of Alerts You'll Receive

CUSTOMER REQUEST (Highest Priority)
Table 7: Service requested
Customer pressed service call button - needs immediate assistance
Response: Under 30 seconds
BEHAVIORAL ALERT (High Priority)
Table 3: Viewed menu 3 times in 5 minutes
Customer likely ready to order - approach when convenient
Response: Within 2 minutes

How to Respond to Alerts

  1. 1
    Read the Alert Carefully
    Understand what the customer needs and the urgency level
  2. 2
    Approach the Table
    Go to the specified table number with appropriate materials (menu, order pad, etc.)
  3. 3
    Address the Need
    Provide the service requested or take their order if they're ready
  4. 4
    Mark as Resolved
    Tap "Resolved" in your mobile dashboard to clear the alert
